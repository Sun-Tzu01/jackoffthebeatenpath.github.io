Day 1 – Springer Mountain

Georgia

Date: March 15, 2027
Miles Today: 8.1
Total Trail Miles: 8.1
Camp: Hawk Mountain Shelter

Today was the beginning of a journey I've been thinking about for years. Standing at Springer Mountain and seeing the first white blaze felt surreal.

The weather was cool and clear, making for an ideal first day. The climbs felt tougher than expected with a full pack, but the excitement of finally being on trail carried me through.

I met several other northbound hikers and shared dinner at camp. Everyone seems to have a different story and reason for being here, which is already becoming one of my favorite parts of the experience.

Sunrise at Springer Mountain before beginning the hike.

Gear Notes

The pack felt comfortable overall, though I may need to adjust the shoulder straps. Water filtration worked perfectly.

Tomorrow's Goal

Reach Gooch Mountain Shelter and settle into a trail routine.
