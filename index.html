Welcome

Welcome to the official, often replicated, never duplicated, Appalachian Trail thru-hike website owned by yours truly, Jack.

This website serves to supplement my documentation of the journey southbound from Katahdin to Springer Mountain. As I walk 2200 miles across 14 different states, this website will see periodic updates from photos and journaling on the trail. Alongside journal entries, I hope to add photos and other artistic touches to the site as the experience comes to life.

Keep scrolling down to check out my current location - provided via satellite! P.S. Make sure to clear all map filters to see the tags from all the places I have walked through!
