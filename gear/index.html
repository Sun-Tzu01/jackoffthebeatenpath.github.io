Appalachian Trail Gear List

The equipment I will be carrying during my 2026 southbound Appalachian Trail thru-hike. Note that this is all as of departing, and updates to the list, as well as final markups and review of gear will be published upon completion of the trail.

Base Weight

Current Base Weight: 19.6 lbs

Base weight does not include food, water, or consumables.

Shelter System

Tent

Brand / Model: Six Moon Designs Lunar Solo

Weight: 1lb 9.94 oz

Notes: Chosen for being lightweight with some space. Has nice vestibule for storing shoes/pack.

Tent Misc. Gear

Brand / Model: Six Moon Designs Tent Stakes and Six Moon Designs Groundsheet

Weight: Stakes 2.59 oz, Goundsheet 4.45oz

Notes: Chosen for being an integrated system. Easy to order w/ tent.

Trekking Poles

Brand / Model: Cascade Mountain Tech Trekking Poles

Weight: 1lb 5 oz

Notes: Cheap. One of these is used for tent pole.

Sleep System

Sleeping Bag

Brand / Model: Kelty Cosmic 20

Temperature Rating: 20°F

Weight: 2 lbs 9.28 oz

Notes: Warm, relatively inexpensive and light. Decent tradeoffs between each.

Sleeping Pad

Brand / Model: Thermarest Z-Lite

Weight: 14 oz

Notes: Opted for CCF over inflatable pad to avoid popping issues, simplify packing

Backpack

Pack

Brand / Model: Osprey Exos 58

Capacity: 58 liters

Weight: 2 lbs 14.72 oz

Notes: Opted for a heavier pack with a better frame to save back and make heavy carries easier.

Cooking System

Stove

Brand / Model: MSR PocketRocket 2

Fuel: Isobutane

Weight: 3.69 oz

Notes: Used prior, reliable.

Cookware

Items:

  • Pot - Toaks 750 ML 3.95 oz
  • Spoon - Toaks Titanium Spork 0.65 oz
  • Lighter - bic (2) w/ tape 1.8 oz

Water System

Water Filter

Brand / Model: Sawyer Squeeze

Weight: 3.66 oz

Notes: Simple, reliable, easy to use and replace.

Electronics

Garmin inReach Mini2

Purpose: Satellite communication and live location tracking.

Phone / Battery System

Notes: iPhone 11 w/ 10,000 mAh rechargeable battery pack

Safety & Miscellaneous
